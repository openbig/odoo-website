Website Lead Snippet

Generate structured leads from your website

This website lead snippet module allows to generate tagged leads from your website. As a website author you can use two contact form snippets wherever you want on your website. Therefore it is possible to position many adopted anchors to catch the right leads from your website.

The autamtically applied lead categories can be used to proceed specific workflows for your sales teams or salesmen.

Define your lead categories

At first use the core odoo functionality to create tags for your leads. This tags will be dispayed later on, if you want to put the contact snippet to your website and let you decide which tag should be applied automatically.

Unse the building block "Contact Eval"

If you edit your website click on "Insert Blocks", choose from the content sub-menu the building block "Contact Eval" or "Contact Eval Ex.". The first one adds two fields to your contact form, the second one adds to the email adress and name further more the company and the phone number.

Contracts in sales menu

If you drop one of the contact form building blocks to various areas of your website an assistance asks you to choose the tag you want to apply if somebody fills the form.
